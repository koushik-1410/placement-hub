For all your placement needs

The website provides a range of functionalities to help students identify company placements based on their branch and placement records. Users can securely sign up or log in to access personalized features. They can filter companies by their branch, search using advanced options like company name, package, year, or job roles, and view detailed placement statistics, including the number of students placed, average packages, and job roles offered. The platform also allows users to compare companies based on placement trends, bookmark or save preferred records, and access additional preparation resources such as interview tips and FAQs. With its user-friendly interface and responsive design, the website ensures a seamless experience across all devices.
